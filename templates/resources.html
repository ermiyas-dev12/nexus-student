Finding Safe Accommodation

How to avoid rental scams, understand your rental agreement, and comply with registration requirements.

1
Use Avito, Cian, or your university housing services
2
Do not pay before seeing the apartment in person or via video
3
Sign a written rental agreement with the landlord
4
Register your address within 7 working days — mandatory by law
Work Permits

Working While Studying

Understand your legal work conditions as an international student in Russia.

1
You can work only with a valid work permit or if employed by your university
2
Work is restricted to the region where your university is located
3
Apply for a work permit through authorities or with university assistance
4
After graduation, you must change to a work visa or residence permit to continue working

2

Carry your insurance documents when seeking treatment
